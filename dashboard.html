Hedge recommendation: Buy 11,377 contracts of the Kalshi unemployment market (e.g., "Will US unemployment exceed 7.0%?"). At $0.30 per contract, total upfront cost is $3,413. Each contract pays $1 if the event occurs, $0 otherwise — so if unemployment exceeds the threshold, you receive $11,377 to offset income loss.
